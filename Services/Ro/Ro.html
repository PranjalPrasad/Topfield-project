How It Works

The RO Purification Process

Our multi-stage reverse osmosis process removes up to 99% of dissolved solids, bacteria and contaminants.

01
🪨

Pre-Filtration

Pressure sand & activated carbon filters remove suspended particles, sediment and chlorine, protecting the RO membrane.

02
🧪

Chemical Dosing

Anti-scalant and SMBS dosing prevents membrane scaling and biofouling for long service life and sustained efficiency.

03
💧

RO Membrane

High-rejection semi-permeable membranes in MS/FRP vessels strip dissolved salts, heavy metals and pathogens.

04
☀️

UV Disinfection

UV lamps eliminate 99.99% of remaining bacteria and viruses, delivering safe potable water at the point of use.
