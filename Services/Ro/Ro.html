How It Works

The RO Purification Process

Our multi-stage reverse osmosis process removes up to 99% of dissolved solids, bacteria and contaminants.

1
Pre-Filtration
Sand & carbon filters remove sediment and chlorine, protecting the RO membrane.
2
Chemical Dosing
Anti-scalant & SMBS dosing prevents membrane scaling and biofouling.
3
RO Membrane
High-rejection membranes strip dissolved salts, heavy metals and pathogens.
4
UV Disinfection
UV lamps eliminate 99.99% of remaining bacteria and viruses from purified water.
5
Pure Water Ready
Safe, mineralised potable water delivered at the point of use — tested and certified.
