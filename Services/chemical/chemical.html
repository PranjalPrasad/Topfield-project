Sodium Hypochlorite (NaOCl)

Anti-Scalant

Prevents mineral scale formation on RO membranes from calcium carbonate, sulphate and silica deposits — extending membrane life significantly.

SMBS (Sodium Meta-Bisulphite)

Dechlorinating agent used to neutralise residual chlorine before RO membranes, protecting the thin-film composite from oxidative degradation.

RO Pre-treatment Dechlorination

PAC (Poly Aluminium Chloride)

High-efficiency coagulant for turbidity removal in water treatment and sewage treatment plants. Faster settling than conventional alum.

WTP STP Coagulation

Culture (Biological Media)

Bacterial culture for seeding biological STP systems — accelerates startup of MBBR, activated sludge and other biological treatment processes.

STP Startup MBBR Biological

06

TCC (Trichloro Cyanuric Acid)

Slow-dissolving chlorine tablet for sustained disinfection of swimming pools and water bodies, maintaining consistent free chlorine levels.

Swimming Pool Water Bodies

Granular Activated Carbon (GAC)

Adsorbs chlorine, organic compounds and taste & odour compounds in water — used in pressure carbon filters as pre- and post-treatment.
