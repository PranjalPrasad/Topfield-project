How We Work

Our Refurbishment Process

A structured 5-step methodology ensuring every repair is thorough, lasting and delivers measurable improvement in plant performance.

01
🔍

Site Assessment

Detailed inspection of all equipment, piping, instrumentation and water quality to identify every fault and inefficiency.

02
📋

Diagnosis & Report

Root-cause analysis report with prioritised action plan, bill of materials and cost estimate presented to the client.

03
🔧

Repair & Upgrade

Replacement of worn parts, media, membranes and vessels. Controls upgraded where necessary for better automation.

04
🧪

Testing & QC

Comprehensive water quality testing and performance benchmarking against design parameters before handover.

05
✅

Recommission & AMC

Full plant recommissioning with operator training and optional Annual Maintenance Contract for ongoing peace of mind.

The Difference

Before & After Refurbishment

See exactly what changes when Topfield takes over an ageing, underperforming water treatment plant.

⚠ Before
🏚️

Deteriorated Plant

  • Fouled RO membranes, poor permeate quality
  • Corroded MS vessels and pipe fittings
  • Failed pumps, blowers and dosing systems
  • Saturated filter media — no filtration
  • Manual operation, no automation or alarms
  • Non-compliant effluent discharge
  • High energy consumption and breakdowns
→
✓ After
🏆

Restored Plant

  • Fresh membranes, high-purity treated water
  • New FRP/MS vessels, clean pipework
  • Reconditioned pumps, blowers fully operational
  • Fresh media — full filtration capacity restored
  • Automated control panel with alarms
  • Compliant output — pollution board ready
  • Optimised energy use, minimum downtime
